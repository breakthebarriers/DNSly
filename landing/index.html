Features

Create, edit, import, and export connection profiles. Each profile stores your full tunnel configuration — server, port, credentials, and cipher.

How it works

Up and running
in three steps

No technical knowledge required. DNSly handles all the complexity so you can focus on browsing freely.

1
Import or Create a Profile
Scan a QR code, paste a slipnet:// link, or manually configure a new connection profile with your server details.
2
Select Your Tunnel
Pick the tunnel type and DNS transport that matches your network environment — VayDNS, SSH, SOCKS5, DoH, or DoT.
3
Tap Connect
Hit the connect button and you're live. DNSly sets up the VPN profile and establishes your encrypted tunnel automatically.

Security

Engineered for
high-risk environments

Every connection is encrypted end-to-end. DNSly is built for users who need real privacy, not just a VPN label.

Kill Switch All traffic is blocked if the tunnel drops — no accidental exposure.
AES-256-GCM & ChaCha20-Poly1305 Industry-standard SSH ciphers protect your tunnel data.
Encrypted Profile Storage Profiles are locked locally. Credentials never leave your device unencrypted.
DNS-over-HTTPS / DNS-over-TLS Your DNS queries are encrypted so ISPs can't see what you're resolving.
Auto Connect Reconnect on app launch so you're never accidentally unprotected.
